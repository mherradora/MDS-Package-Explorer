R Multidimensional Scaling Ecosystem

This application is an explorer for R packages on CRAN dedicated to Multidimensional Scaling (MDS). At the core of the ecosystem is smacof, which implements the majorization algorithm for metric and non-metric MDS. Other packages extend its functionality or apply it to specific domains (e.g. genetics, asymmetric distances, and optimization).
